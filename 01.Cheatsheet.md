## **Indexing Vectors**

Vectors in R are **1-indexed**. You can use numeric or logical indexing:

```r
v[1]        # 10
v[-4]       # 10 21 32
v[c(2, 3)]  # 21 32
v[-c(2, 3)] # 10 47
v[v > 25]   # 32 47
